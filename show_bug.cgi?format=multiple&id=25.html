Description Gary Holmlund 2013-04-06 18:11:44 EEST 
Received from the Sword BibleTime forum:

I use Korora 18 based on Fedora 18. I have kde 4.10.2 installed on my machine (32-bit). I upgraded to qtwebkit 2.3 and bibletime was no longer displaying any bibles or text. In yum I downgraded to qtwebkit 2.2 and everything is working again.

Hope this helps someone out there because I had to find this out for myself and isolate the problem. Nothing was on the internet about this.

God bless, smile

Comment 1 Gary Holmlund 2013-04-06 18:58:40 EEST 
Release announcement for QtWebKit 2.3
http://blogs.kde.org/2013/03/15/qtwebkit-230-out

Some excerpts:
Note that QtWebKit 2.3 is not an official Qt release, nor will be. We recommend users to upgrade to Qt 5, but for those stuck with Qt 4.8 for the time being and who is using QtWebKit, I would personally recommend trying out QtWebKit 2.3. I have also had great feedback from the developers of the Rekonq, and Qupzilla browsers, that also recommend users to try out 2.3, several distributions are either packaging or planning to package it, the first being Arch Linux who has been packaging QtWebKit 2.3 since the betas.
